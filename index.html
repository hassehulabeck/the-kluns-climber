The Kluns climber

Help Tristan to climb to the top by winning duels of Sten-sax-påse ("Klunsa") against the dreaded foes.

Use keys S, X and P to choose (S)ten, sa(X) eller (P)åse.

Every duel is won by getting first to 5 wins. Win to advance, lose to fall back.

Game over when health is 0.
